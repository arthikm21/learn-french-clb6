Verb: avoir (to have) — Present

A clear, example-first explanation built for CLB 6 / B1 — the level you need for TCF / TEF Canada.

Overview

Avoir = to have. Also used for age, hunger, thirst (J'ai 25 ans = I am 25). Critical helper for past tense.

Conjugation

j'ai
tuas
il/elle/ona
nousavons
vousavez
ils/ellesont

Idiomatic uses

French uses avoir where English uses to be.

  • J'ai faim. (I'm hungry)
  • Tu as soif. (You're thirsty)
  • Elle a 30 ans. (She is 30)
  • Il a peur. (He is afraid)
