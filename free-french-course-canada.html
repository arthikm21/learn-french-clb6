A free French course built for Canada

Everything you need to reach CLB 6 on the TCF / TEF Canada — phonics, vocabulary, grammar, listening, speaking, reading, writing, and a full mock test. In native Canadian French. Completely free.

Why a free Canadian French course exists

Most CLB 6 prep is locked behind paid apps, tutoring packages, or textbooks. If you are preparing for Express Entry, a federal job, or professional licensing, you should not have to pay to practice. This course is free, requires no signup, and stores your progress only in your browser — nothing leaves your device.

What's inside

  • Phonics & sounds — minimal-pair ear training: u vs ou, nasals, é vs è, liaison.
  • Vocabulary — themed decks with spaced repetition that schedules your reviews automatically.
  • Grammar — units from A1 to B1, including the four CLB 6 traps solved with decision trees.
  • Listening — graded clips and dictation from slow to natural speed.
  • Speaking — shadowing with pronunciation feedback, plus open-ended TCF speaking tasks.
  • Reading — graded texts from CLB 3 to 6: emails, ads, news, brochures, fiction.
  • Writing — prompts with a grammar checker that flags gender, tense, and elision errors.
  • Full mock test — TCF-format, four skills, with a CLB band estimate at the end.

Native Canadian French — not Parisian

Every phrase plays in a neural Canadian French voice, and the curriculum is calibrated to the CLB 6 ceiling: no literary tenses, no passé simple — only the French an immigrant in Quebec or a professional in a francophone workplace actually uses.
