A free CLB 6 French course for Canada

Bonjour! is a free, no-signup French course built for one outcome: reaching CLB 6 (roughly B1) across all four skills on the TCF Canada and TEF Canada exams. It is made for Express Entry candidates, federal job seekers, and anyone who needs Canadian Language Benchmark 6 in French — with native Canadian French audio, a full exam-format mock test, and no tracking.

What you can practise

Phonics and minimal-pair ear training, themed vocabulary with spaced repetition, grammar from A1 to B1, graded listening and reading, speaking shadowing with pronunciation feedback, a writing grader, and the exact TCF Canada speaking and writing task formats — all ordered into an eight-phase path with gate quizzes between phases.
